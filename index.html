About me

I feel into product management after many years of technical support; I moved to leading implementations for electronic health record systems for medium-large specialty clinical practices. While implementing EHRs, I moved to leading and managing the product I was implementing. My experience with deploying and maintaining an EHR product brought me to Phoenix where I am currently leading a reimagination of a Behavioral Health EHR system in an industry thirsting for technology. A core value of mine to be a relentless learner, I decided to hone my technical craft a bit more. In February I enrolled, and I am currently in the throws of a M.E.R.N. Full-Stack Continuing Education Coding Program. I am also a Husband, a Father of two precious girls, and a huge movie buff.
