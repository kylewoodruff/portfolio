About me

After many years of working in technical support, I moved into product management. I lead implementations for electronic health record systems for medium to large specialty clinical practices. While implementing EHRs, I transitioned to leading and managing the product. My experience with deploying and maintaining an EHR product prepared me to come to Phoenix where I am currently driving a reimagination of a Behavioral Health EHR system in an industry thirsting for technology. A core value of mine is to be a relentless learner, and as such only a few months after graduating with my MBA, I decided to hone my technical craft more intently. In February I enrolled in a M.E.R.N. Full-Stack Continuing Education Coding Program and am still in the throws of this demanding six-month course. I am loving every minute of the 20+ hours a week that I am spending fully immersed in code. I am also a husband, father of two precious girls, and a huge movie buff.
